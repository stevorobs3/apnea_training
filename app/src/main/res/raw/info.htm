Practice holding your breath. CO2 and O2 Tables

Very often, people who have completed a training course freediving, ask how You can train your breath hold home away from the sea, in order to better prepare for the next trip and to not lose shape.

Quite effective and popular among freedivers training, allowing significantly increase the length of breath, is a static delay training on CO2 and O2 tables. What is it and what does it do?

Hypercapnia table CO2

As is well known, the main trigger for inhalation is the level of carbon dioxide in the lungs. With breath-hold and increase the amount of carbon dioxide, our brain sends a signal to the respiratory system to breathe. Thus breath holding divided into 2 phases: phase comfort and phase of the struggle, when the brain sends signals to the active respiratory system, and she begins answer that diaphragm contraction (contractions).

Training consists of a series of breathing delay, aimed at developing the body's tolerance to higher CO2 levels (Hypercapnia), increasing carbon dioxide levels during each subsequent delay. For this purpose, the delay time is breathing constant, and the time delays between the rest is gradually reduced, thereby increasing the residual level of CO2 for each subsequent delay.

It is recommended to select the delay time between 50% and 80% of its maximum. The start time of rest for 2-3 minutes. The interval to reduce the time of rest for 15-20 seconds.

Hypoxic table O2

Series breathing delay, aimed at developing the body's tolerance to low oxygen levels (hypoxia). This is achieved by a gradual an increase in the duration of breath at the same time relaxing. rest interval should be large enough to catch to get rid of excessive levels of CO2, accumulated after the delay and recover. Delay interval large enough to receive hypoxic required load, i.e. delay the end of the table should be close to maximum.

We remind that all the independent exercise on respiratory arrest spending only on land! The first rule of free diving - Never dive alone, refers including to the static breath hold.

For convenience, we recommend that you use the clip for the nose, and to track and fix the results of purchase oximeter. You can create a table in which to record important parameters such as heart rate, blood oxygen saturation oxygen contractions start time, maximum delay time, etc. This will analyze your results, and see progress It will create an additional incentive for training.

When you breath in the time of one of the important principles - not to think about time. Therefore, training tables is recommended to use special applications for your phone, which configure the necessary parameters of training, after which you are comfortable is located on the bed, put near the phone, and the app tells you all the instructions.
